Commom Symptoms Of Pancreatic Cancer

Jaundice

Yellowing of skin or eyes due to bile duct blockage.

Weight Loss

Sudden and unexplained weight loss.

New-Onset Diabetes

Diabetes appearing suddenly in adults may indicate risk.

Abdominal Pain

Persistent pain or discomfort in the stomach area.

Family History

Relatives diagnosed with pancreatic cancer increase risk.

Back Pain

Persistent pain in the upper or middle back, which may be a symptom of pancreatic issues.

About us

Promoting Awareness,
Enabling Early Detection

Welcome to PancreaticGuard — a user-friendly web tool that helps identify early symptoms related to pancreatic cancer. By analyzing health-related inputs like diabetes, jaundice, and weight loss, it offers useful insights for awareness. The system supports early action by showing predictions based on medical symptom data. Our aim is to promote health awareness and make early detection more accessible. It's simple to use and designed to assist, not replace medical advice.
